REKM Carjacking Survival Seminar

Posted by REKM on September 29, 2014

According to the US Department of State, CARJACKING has become one of the most prevalent crimes in many parts of the world. Most carjackings occur for the sole purpose of taking the car; it is a crime without a political agenda. As LAPD officials explain, carjackers look for opportunities – they don’t choose victims by sex, race, or age. Think you can’t be a victim? Think again. Here in LA County alone, a 29-year-old male was held at knife point this past Saturday, September 27th by four alleged carjacking suspects. Another man in his 30s was in critical condition this past August after being stabbed repeatedly and struck by his own vehicle in Lancaster during an apparent carjacking. What would you do in this situation? Join the team at REKM for its “REKM CarJacking Survival Seminar” October 12th (Sunday), 2014. Former IDF (Israeli Defense Forces) Krav Maga Head Instructor Roy Elghanayan (4th DAN) will take you through the steps for both prevention and responses during an attack with a knife (as most recent incidences occurred with knives). We will cover knife attacks both inside and outside your vehicle. You don’t want to miss this exciting opportunity that can SAVE YOUR LIFE!
